In the age of technology and social media, most companies are turning to e-marketing, as it is easier and more economical. To expand their reach, many local companies and businesses rely on their social media accounts for marketing and communicating with customers. Although we all use social media, using it in a brand marketing can be a difficult challenge, especially when activating more than one platform. And this is because of the difficulty of following up on all platforms and solving their problems.
